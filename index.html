About Carmel By The Sea Ministry

A place of refuge where grief meets grace.

Carmel By The Sea Ministry was born from a deep desire to walk alongside those experiencing loss, separation or the heavy waves of sorrow. Inspired by biblical places of encounter with God — like Mount Carmel — and the peaceful beauty of coastal refuges. We provide confidential prayer support, scriptural encouragement and practical resources for healing.

Whether you're in the early days of shock or years into the journey, you are not alone. Our team prays with and for you, offering a listening ear grounded in the hope of the Gospel.
